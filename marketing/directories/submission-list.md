## Submission Template

**Product Name:** Leadivo

**Tagline:** The e-commerce platform built for COD and WhatsApp markets

**Category:** E-commerce Platform / Shopify Alternative

**Description (short):**
Leadivo is a free e-commerce platform for sellers in markets where Cash on Delivery dominates and WhatsApp is the primary communication channel. Features include WhatsApp abandoned checkout recovery, multi-currency support, city-level shipping zones, and Arabic/French/English storefronts.

**Description (long):**
Leadivo is a multi-tenant e-commerce platform designed for sellers in MENA, North Africa, and other emerging markets. Unlike Shopify or WooCommerce, Leadivo is built COD-first — the entire workflow assumes Cash on Delivery. The platform includes automatic WhatsApp abandoned checkout recovery (98% open rate vs 20% for email), multi-market pricing with automatic currency conversion, city-level shipping zones with exclusions, a discount system with per-customer limits, and fully localized storefronts in Arabic (RTL), French, and English. Free to start with no credit card required.

**Alternatives to list against:** Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Wix eCommerce, Squarespace Commerce, Ecwid

**Keywords:** ecommerce platform, shopify alternative, cod ecommerce, whatsapp abandoned cart, multi-tenant ecommerce, arabic ecommerce, mena ecommerce
