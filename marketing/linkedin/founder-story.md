I spent months building an e-commerce platform that nobody in Silicon Valley would fund.

And that's exactly why it works.

Here's the thing: 90% of orders in MENA and North Africa are Cash on Delivery. Customers don't have credit cards. They communicate on WhatsApp, not email. They shop from Instagram's in-app browser on a phone.

Shopify doesn't serve these sellers well. Neither does WooCommerce. Neither does anyone, really.

So I built Leadivo.

It's an e-commerce platform that's:
- COD-first (not a bolt-on)
- WhatsApp-integrated (abandoned cart recovery via WhatsApp, not email)
- Multi-currency (sell to France, Morocco, and Algeria from one dashboard)
- Arabic + French + English (full RTL support)
- Free to start

The feature sellers love most? Abandoned checkout recovery via WhatsApp.

When a customer fills in their phone but doesn't complete the order, Leadivo automatically sends a WhatsApp message after 30 minutes with a link to finish their purchase.

WhatsApp open rates: 98%
Email open rates: 20%

The math speaks for itself.

Building for "underserved" markets isn't charity — it's smart business. Low competition, high need, and grateful customers who spread the word.

If you're a small seller looking for a free Shopify alternative, or you know someone who is, check out Leadivo: https://www.leadivo.app
