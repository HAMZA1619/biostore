## Introduction Message (after 1-2 weeks of participation)

**For #introductions or #show-your-work channels:**

Hey everyone! I'm building Leadivo — an e-commerce platform for sellers in COD (Cash on Delivery) markets.

Most e-commerce platforms are built for credit card + email markets. But in MENA, North Africa, and other emerging markets:
- 90%+ of orders are COD
- Customers use WhatsApp, not email
- Sellers need Arabic/French storefronts

So I built a platform that does this natively — including automatic WhatsApp abandoned checkout recovery (98% open rate).

Stack: Next.js 16, Supabase, Tailwind CSS 4, Vercel.

Free to try: https://www.leadivo.app

Happy to share more about building for emerging markets if anyone's curious!
