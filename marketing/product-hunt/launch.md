## Description

Leadivo is a free e-commerce platform built for sellers in markets where Cash on Delivery is king and WhatsApp is the main communication channel.

**Why we built this:**
Shopify is great for credit card markets, but 90%+ of orders in MENA, North Africa, and many emerging markets are COD. Sellers in these regions manage everything through WhatsApp, need Arabic/French storefronts, and can't justify $39/mo when their average order is $15-25.

**Key features:**

- **WhatsApp abandoned checkout recovery** — Automatically sends customers a WhatsApp message when they abandon their cart, with a link to complete their order. 98% open rate vs 20% for email.

- **Cash on Delivery first** — The entire platform is designed around COD workflows, not bolted on as an afterthought.

- **Multi-market pricing** — Sell in multiple countries with automatic currency conversion, per-market pricing, and city-level shipping zones.

- **Mobile-first storefront** — Optimized for Instagram/TikTok in-app browsers, where most traffic comes from in these markets.

- **Arabic + French + English** — Fully localized storefronts with RTL support.

- **Free to start** — No credit card required.

Built with Next.js 16, Supabase, and Tailwind CSS.
